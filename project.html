Urban / Documentary

NAIROBI NOIR

keyboard_arrow_down
Location

Nairobi, Kenya

Year

2025

Service

Photogaphy, Creative Direction

An exploration of the city's duality - where the organic chaos of nature meets the structured rhythm of urban life.

Nairobi is a city of layers. Beneath the concrete and the commotion lies a pulse that is distinctly primal, a reminder of the land that held us long before the skyscrapers did.

"Nairobi Noir" is an attempt to strip away the color and noise, focusing strictly on light, shadow, and form. Shot entirely in black and white (with subtle color grading in post), the series looks at the interplay between the city's inhabitants and its rapidly changing environment.

From the misty mornings in Karura Forest to the frenetic energy of the CBD at dusk, we aimed to capture the silent moments that often go unnoticed. The mood is intentionally somber yet hopeful, reflecting the resilience of the people who call this metropolis home.
