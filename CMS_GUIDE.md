## ğŸ“¸ Managing Your Portfolio (Gallery)

Scale your portfolio as you shoot new masterpieces.

### Adding a New Project

1. Click **Gallery** on the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Pencil icon** (Create new document) at the top right.
3. Fill in the details:
    * **Title**: The name of the project/shoot (e.g., "Serengeti Golden Hour").
    * **Slug**: Click "Generate" to create a URL-friendly version automatically.
    * **Category**: Choose from the dropdown (Portrait, Wedding, Editorial, etc.).
    * **Cover Image**: Upload the main hero image for the card.
        * *Tip*: Use high-quality JPEGs, max 2MB for speed.
    * **Gallery Images**: Upload all the photos for this project.
        * You can drag-and-drop multiple images at once!
        * Add captions if desired.
    * **Location**: Where was this shot? (e.g., "Maasai Mara, Kenya").
    * **Date**: When did this happen? (Optional, helps sorting).
4. **Publish**: Click the green "Publish" button at the bottom right to make it live instantly!
